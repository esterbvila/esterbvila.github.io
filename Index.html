My Journey

February 2020

I finish my degree studies in Advertising & PR and I'm quite lost. What should I do next ? What do I want ? What do I like ?...

I start reading, meditating and writing a lot. Eventually I get to understand more things about myself and how my mind works.

November 2021

I'm admitted into the Frontend web development Bootcamp by BIT Academy. I'm excited to start diving into this sector and be able to master the tools.

While doing HTML & CSS, my aesthetic sense and detail-oriented skills are jumping for joy. I found myself very focused on the tasks and realising that I could be doing this all day.

Then I start doing JavaScript and frustration overcomes satisfaction. I learn about the importance of persevering and that perfection and comparison with others can be fatal.

March 2021

Having finished the Bootcamp, I'm now on my way of mastering JavaScript and View.
